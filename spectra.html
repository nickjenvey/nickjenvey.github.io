Spectra Wines

I designed the initial theme for Commerce7, a game-changing ecommerce platform in the alcohol industry. The goal was to create a visually appealing design that is user-friendly and displays the platform's functions while emulating a modern vineyard vibe.

Design objectives

The design aimed for:

  • A blend of modern and rustic aesthetics.
  • An inviting, homely ambiance.
  • A seamless integration of platform features with captivating visuals.

Wireframing process

For efficient brainstorming, I used a quick wireframing process. Sketching quickly enabled:

  • Rapid exploration of unique concepts.
  • Jotted down fleeting ideas for later use.
  • Increased creativity by skipping the small details.

Several design iterations were done to highlight all of the platform's key features. Each revision improved the design, highlighting its unique functions and user customization options. This thorough approach ensured that nothing was overlooked.

Results

The concept fit the envisioned winery experience. Inspired by vineyard sunsets, I chose a warm colour palette of purples, oranges, and yellows. I also created the company logo, wine bottles, and name, demonstrating my varied creativity.

The most important platform features were highlighted. The user experience was improved via intuitive navigation, appealing product displays, and a smooth checkout process.
