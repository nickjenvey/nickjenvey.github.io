Spectra Wines

I designed the first theme for Commerce7, a groundbreaking ecommerce platform in the alcohol sector. The mission: a visually striking design that's user-friendly and highlights the platform's features, encapsulating a contemporary winery feel.

Design objectives

The design aimed for:

  • A blend of modern and rustic aesthetics.
  • An inviting, homely ambiance.
  • A seamless integration of platform features with captivating visuals.

Wireframing process

I adopted a swift wireframing method for efficient ideation. Quick sketching:

  • Allowed for diverse concept exploration.
  • Jotted down fleeting ideas for later use.
  • Increased creativity by skipping the small details.

Multiple layout drafts ensured all essential features found their place.

Results

The theme resonated with the envisioned winery experience. Drawing inspiration from vineyard sunsets, I selected a warm color palette with purples, oranges, and yellows. I also designed the brand logo, wine bottles, and named the brand, underscoring my multifaceted creativity.

Key platform features were given prominence. Intuitive navigation, attractive product displays, and smooth checkouts enhanced user experience.
